Asset Lifecycle: Trees vs. Grey Infrastructure

Comparing Appreciating Living Assets vs. Depreciating Structural Assets

The Only Appreciating Asset

Standard infrastructure—roofs, asphalt, HVAC systems—begins to depreciate the moment it is installed. Urban trees are the inverse. As they grow, their ecosystem services (shading, stormwater management) compound, increasing their appraised value every year.

UV Protection

Energy Mitigation
